Why With Us?

La Crosse has the highest concentration of bars on one street in the nation, which is shocking considering we have a population of a mere 55,000 people. We also have two huge breweries that supply alot of alcohol to the midwest and the rest of the country, as well as the worlds largest six pack. Octoberfest in La Crosse is the second biggest in the world, second only to Berlin, Germany. That being said, we know our drinking which is why icapthat© provides the perfect blend of La Crosse's rich culture with a crafty twist.

icapthat© is a way for us to support smaller businesses local to La Crosse while continuing our education and grow as independent businessmen. Starting icapthat© has given us a little insight into the world of entreprenurship while still doing something that we're passionate about.

At icapthat©, we are all about building and designing quality cap inspiried decor and home accessories to bring unique talking points to any home.

Many of the caps used in the creation of our products come from the 138 craft breweries found here in Wisconsin! We'd like to give a big thank you to each brewery for letting us express our creativity through your aesthetically appealing bottle caps.

icapthat© specializes in building and designing custom cap themed furniture and home accessories. Each product is hand made by us here in La Crosse, WI and local orders will be delievered.
