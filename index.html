Why With Us?

La Crosse is a city in Wisconsin. La Crosse has the most bars on one street in the whole nation (thats crazy considering we only have 55,000 people), two huge breweries that supply alot of alcohol to the midwest and the rest of the country, and the worlds largest six pack. Alcohol prices are really low, and our bars always have cheap drinks and really good specials. The Octoberfest in La Crosse is the second biggest in the world, second only to Berlin, Germany. That being said, we know our drinking which is why i'd cap that© provides the perfect blend of La Crosse's rich culture with a crafty twist.
